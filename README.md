# CSE357: Combinatorial Studies
### Master Course Dashboard (Session 2025-26)

Welcome to the ultimate preparation portal for **CSE357: Combinatorial Studies**. This course is designed to solidify your computer science fundamentals, algorithm design skills, and software engineering concepts, thoroughly preparing you for technical and behavioral interviews at product- and service-oriented companies.

---

## 🎯 Course Outcomes (COs)
*   **CO1**: Understand fundamental computer science concepts (Data Structures, Algorithms, Databases, OS, Computer Networks).
*   **CO2**: Assess problem-solving skills specific to coding challenges and algorithmic problems frequently encountered in technical interviews.
*   **CO3**: Articulate a comprehensive command of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) principles.
*   **CO4**: Gain comprehensive strategies and techniques to effectively prepare for resumes, mock interviews, and communications.
*   **CO5**: Confidence and skills to address interview inquiries posed by IT services companies.
*   **CO6**: Whiteboard coding, mock simulations, and behavioral interview scenarios.
